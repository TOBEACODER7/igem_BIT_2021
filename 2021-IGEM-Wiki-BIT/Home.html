Early screening of colorectal
cancer(CRC) based on
CRISPR/Cas12-coupled SlipChip
for detection of miRNA
       
Introduction:
Early screening and treatment of CRC has been proved to be conducive to reducing the mortality rate of CRC. Currently, most diagnostic methods do not meet the requirements of rapid, sensitive and specific screening of CRC. We have developed an early screening system for CRC with the above advantages.
