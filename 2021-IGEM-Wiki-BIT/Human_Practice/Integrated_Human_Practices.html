MODULE1 Background investigation for project establishment
Literature research and brain storm
In the project topic selection stage, we learned about CRC (CRC) through literature research and group discussions. According to the survey, we know that improving the proportion of early diagnosis is considered to be the key to improving the survival rate of CRC patients, and early screening of CRC is particularly important in clinical practice. Based on the above research results, we completed the preliminary topic selection of the project and discussed our project with Professor PI Deng Yulin, Associate Professor Lv Xuefei and Professor Li Xiaoqiong. The three professors proposed the following guidance for the project:
1. First of all, the professors conducted a feasibility analysis of the project based on our research on the background and related detection methods of CRC and the basis of the project, which promoted us to determine the project for early screening of CRC;
2. secondly, through our report, the professors also pointed out the shortcomings of our current research : the integrity of the project is not enough; more inclined to the basic background of the research; there is no specific research on how to realize the specific methods of early detection of CRC.

Based on our survey on the background of CRC, we prepared a questionnaire on the basic situation of CRC. The goal of the questionnaire is to count people ’ s understanding of CRC and the acceptability of CRC screening. A total of 107 questionnaires were received. The personal information of the respondents was protected.

Sense of Value
According to the data background investigation, we know that the 5-year survival rate of young patients with rectal cancer aged 20-40 years old and elderly patients with rectal cancer aged 60-80 years old in stage I, II, III and IV has a significant decreasing trend. Most patients with CRC have reached the middle and late stages of the tumor at the time of diagnosis, so that patients miss the best treatment period and about 15 % ~ 25 % of them cried for liver metastasis at the time of diagnosis. More than 80 % of the liver metastases can not be radical resection. Therefore, improving the proportion of early diagnosis is considered to be the key to improving the survival rate of patients with colorectal cancer. Early screening of CRC is particularly important in clinical practice. At the same time, in our questionnaire, the masses are extremely expecting for the early screening of CRC, because based on the early screening of CRC, it can help to detect tumors in the early stage and remove local cancerous sites or precancerous lesions ( adenomatous polyps ) to effectively prevent and improve the occurrence and prognosis of CRC. The stage diagnosis of CRC can greatly improve the survival rate of patients, thereby reducing the occurrence of tumor metastasis. Our project has carried out a systematic experimental design based on the early screening of colorectal cancer and the product is realized to make our contribution to the early screening of CRC. Put forward our ideas and contributions for early screening of CRC, effectively prevent and improve the occurrence of CRC, and cooperate with doctors to make early diagnosis of colorectal cancer, greatly improve the survival rate of patients.
