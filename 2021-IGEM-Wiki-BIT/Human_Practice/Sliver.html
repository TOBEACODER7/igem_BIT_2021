Silver
This year our project aims at early diagnosis and treatment of CRC. Based on the consideration of the impact of COVID-19 prevention and the convenience of big data on the Internet, we insists on "online and offline ", "interview and return visit", and "educate and being educated" in the education field in 2021. We conducted research on relevant literature, in-depth public questionnaire visits to understand, to solicit opinions from experts and scholars, return visits to the masses and other activities. During the masses and other activities, in the course of these activities, we complete the project design, implementation and summary, combined with human practice to obtain feedback, in order to promote and improve our project. This can help us consider the future plan of the project so that we can better serve the society.
Thinking and Discussion
In the project setting stage, our team members learned about colorectal cancer (CRC) through literature research, group discussions and other measures. We conducted a detailed background survey and data analysis on the improvement of gastrointestinal cancer. Based on the research results, we discussed our project with Professor PI Deng Yulin, associate professor Lv Xuefei and professor Li Xiaoqiong. Under the guidance and suggestions of the professors, we completed the preliminary topic selection of the project.

Fig1：Regular meeting exchange report

Fig2：Brainstorm

After the report, team BIT began to conduct literature research and regular meeting discussions and carried out brainstorming according to the research results. It was determined that the goal of this year’ s project was to achieve early diagnosis and treatment of colorectal cancer and to design a low-cost, high-sensitivity and high-throughput colorectal cancer marker detection system. At the same time, the team completed the assignment of responsibilities, and set the biological group, hardware group and modeling group, laying the foundation for the subsequent project.
Questionnaire investigation
In order to count people’s understanding of CRC and the acceptability of CRC screening, we designed a questionnaire for the general public on the basic situation of CRC. We received a total of 107 questionnaires, protected the personal information of those who filled out the questionnaires, and performed statistical analysis on the questionnaires received, which played a guiding role in our project design and HP development.

Fig3,4： Questionnaire results

These data prove that our project has realistic demand and basic audiences, which makes us confident to continue to promote the project. At the same time, the results of the questionnaire also tell us that the relevant popular science propaganda is very necessary.

We also used other online promotions. On the Bilibili platform, team BIT and team YiYe-China conducted a live broadcast of colorectal cancer science promotion and synthetic biology promotion. The live broadcast introduced the clinical symptoms, hazards, mainstream of colorectal cancer, detection methods and more optimized detection methods conceived by the two teams respectively. This live broadcast has nearly 5,000 popularity points (approximately 500 people) watched this live broadcast. The main users of the Bilibili platform are young people aged 14-30. At the same time, the younger age and early onset of colorectal cancer are not well known to the public. Therefore, our live broadcast is aimed at the "target audience". Through this live broadcast, we can let them know more about colorectal cancer. So we can introduce our project to them and broaden the audience base of the product.
In this live broadcast, some researchers study CRC provided us with very valuable opinions during the live broadcast. His suggestions such as "the pros and cons of the current markers and the detection methods used" and "how to solve the problem of false positives" all make us think. Therefore, we also arranged special experiments to solve these problems in the follow-up project improvement. problem. At the same time, he also recommended that we increase the carrier experiment, and after in-depth communication with him, we will also take this as one of our future improvement directions.

Summary
Through literature research, public information feedback and other preliminary research, we have counted the data, so that we can determine that this year ’ s project is of practical significance and is required by the public. Similarly, according to the results of previous research, we also determine that we should carry out popular science lectures on related projects to help more people understand CRC and its detection methods as well as the trend of synthetic biology, and at the same time enter the classroom for the dissemination of synthetic biology.

Communicating with other iGEM teams can also give us experience in projects, as can popular science campaigns with them to promote synthetic biology, iGEM and our projects.
